About Industry Week

Industry Week 2025 is a week-long conference (Sep 29 – Oct 3) that takes place once a year in partnership with major industry actors. This conference is composed of many engaging events such as competitions, panels, workshops, plant and office tours, and a networking event, all organized by IISE Concordia.

These events offer amazing opportunities including a cash prize for the winning case competition team, provided and judged by Tecsys Inc., a major healthcare consulting firm.

Through this IISE Concordia initiative, the association aims at contributing to the advancement of the industrial engineering field and all other connected professional fields as well.
